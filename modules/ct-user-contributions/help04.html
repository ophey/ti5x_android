MM-04 — Dew Point/Wind Chill/Heat Index

This will calculate Wind Chill, Heat Index and Dew Point, in °C or ⁰F.

The Wind Chill is based on the National Weather Service formula found at https://www.weather.gov/ffc/wci, Wind Chill does not exist above 50⁰ or for wind speeds less than 3 mph.

The Dew Point is based on https://www.omnicalculator.com/physics/dew-point#how-to-calculate-dew-point-how-to-calculate-relative-humidity and it is calculated in ⁰C and then converted to ⁰F

The Heat Index formula can be found here https://www.wpc.ncep.noaa.gov/html/heatindex_equation.shtml It is comprised of different formulae each valid under specific circumstances. There is a simple version for temperatures under 80⁰F and a more complex version for higher temperatures, which is further modified for "dry" or "wet" conditions

The Heat Index calculation calculates all versions internally and then selects the proper result based on temperature and relative humidity. To avoid an internal error condition an absolute value |x| term is added.

Instructions

  1. Enter temperature:
    • in ⁰F, press A
      or
    • in ⁰C, press 2nd A'
  2. Enter wind speed
    • in MPH, press B
    • in KM/H, press 2nd B'
  3. Enter relative humidity RH, press C
  4. Select English (0) or Metric (1) outputs, enter 0 or 1, press 2nd E'
  5. Calculate Dew Point, press D
  6. Calculate Wind chill, press C
  7. Calculate Heat Index, press 2nd D'

Example 1

  1. Temperature is 45⁰F, press 45 A.
  2. Wind speed is 10 mph, press 10 B
  3. Relative humidity is 35%, press 35 C
  4. Calculate in Imperial units, press 0 2nd E'
  5. Get Dewpoint, press D, 18.967271
  6. Convert to °C, 2nd C', -7.240404938
  7. Get Windchill, press E, 39.8396758
  8. Convert to °C, 2nd C', 4.35537544
  9. Get Heat Index, press 2nd D', 40.845
  10. Convert to °C, 2nd C', 4.91388889

Example 2

  1. Temperature is 30⁰C, press 30 2nd A'.
  2. Wind speed is 6 km/h, press 6 2nd B'
  3. Relative humidity is 40%, press 40 C
  4. Calculate in Metric units, press 1 2nd E'
  5. Get Dewpoint, press D, 14.932995
  6. Get Windchill, press E, 30
  7. Get Heat Index, press 2nd D', 29.6891651

Registers used: 00 to 23
