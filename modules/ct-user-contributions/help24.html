A 3D Tic-Tac-Toe Game

To play:

  • To initialize press E
  • Enter a square (1 .. 27) press A or R/S
  • The board can be printed at any time by pressing D O's are your pieces and X's are calculator's ones.

If you want the calculator to move first, press 0 and press A or R/S.

The calculator returns its counter move in the display. If that number is flashing, the machine has won.

A flashing display of 99999999 indicates an illegal move.
