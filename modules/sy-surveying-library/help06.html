The closure of a traverse is that line which will exactly close the traverse. Given the calculated and the correct endpoints, this program will compute the closure distance (horizontal), bearing (error bearing) and quadrant, latitude, departure, and precision ratio.

When used in conjunction with the traverse programs, a routine is included to complete calculation of the area of a closed traverse.

  • Select degree mode, press 2nd Deg
  • If used standalone:
    • Enter total distance traversed, press 2nd C'
    • Enter partial area, press 2nd D'
    • Enter sector area, press STO 1 7
    • Enter calculated North, press A
    • Enter calculated East, press A
    • Continue below...
  • If used with a Traverse program, start here:
    • Enter correct North, press B
    • Enter correct East, press B
    • Compute closure distance, press C
    • Compute error bearing (DD.MMSSss), press D
    • Compute quadrant, press E
    • Compute Area, press 2nd A'
    • Compute precision ratio, press 2nd B'

Registers used: 01-07, 09, 10, 13, 16-19
