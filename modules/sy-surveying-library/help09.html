SY-09 â€” Vertical Curve Design

This program computes the stationing and elevation of stations along a vertical curve, given the starting and ending grades, and the curve length (or the rate of change of grade (Δg) per station). Also computed are the rate of change of grade per station (or the curve length) and the stationing and elevation of the maximum or minimum elevation point.

  • Initialize, press 2nd E'
  • Enter starting grade, press A
  • Enter ending grade, press A
  • Enter either:
    • curve length, press B
    • change of grade per station, press 2nd B'
  • Either:
    • Enter Beginning station, press C
    • Enter Elevation of StatB, press C
    • Compute Intersection station, press R/S
    • Compute Elevation of StaI, press R/S
    or
    • Enter Intersection station, press 2nd C'
    • Enter Elevation of StatI, press 2nd C'
    • Compute Beginning station, press R/S
    • Compute Elevation of StaB, press R/S
  • Compute Station at maximum or minimum elevation, press D
  • Compute maximum or minimum elevation, press E
  • To find elevation of any curve station, enter station and press E

Registers used: 01-09, 12, 16
