This program calculates the coordinates of the point of intersection of two lines given the bearing and quadrant of each line, and the coordinates of two points, one on each line.

  • Select the degree mode, press 2nd Deg
  • Initialize, press 2nd E'
  • Enter N1 (point 1), press A
  • Enter E1 (point 1), press A
  • Enter N2 (point 2), press B
  • Enter E2 (point 2), press B
  • Enter Bearing of line 1 (DD.MMSS), press C
  • Enter Quadrant of Brg1, press C
  • Enter Bearing of line 2 (DD.MMSS), press D
  • Enter Quadrant of Brg2, press D
  • Compute, NI, press E
  • Compute, EI, press 2nd A'

Registers used: 01-13
