SY-18 — Intersection - (Bearing / Distance)

Given two intersecting lines and knowing a point on each, plus the bearing and quadrant of one line and the distance from the point on the other line to the point of intersection, this program calculates:

  • The distance between the two known points
  • The azimuth from the first to the second known point
  • The coordinates of the point of intersection

Two solutions are possible for thee point of intersection. The second is found by entering the quuadrant 180° opposed to the first (see below).

  • Select the degree mode, press 2nd Deg
  • Initialize, press 2nd E'
  • Enter N1 (point 1), press A
  • Enter E1 (point 1), press A
  • Enter N2 (point 2), press B
  • Enter E2 (point 2), press B
  • Enter Bearing of line 1 (DD.MMSS), press C
  • Enter Quadrant of Brg1, press D
  • Enter Dist2, press E
  • Compute distance from point 1 to point 2, press 2nd A'
  • Compute azimuth from point 1 to point 2, press 2nd B'
  • Compute, NI, press 2nd C'
  • Compute, EI, press 2nd D'

Registers used: 01-14, 20
