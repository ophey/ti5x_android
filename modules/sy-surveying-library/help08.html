SY-07, SY-08 form a group and are to be used together.

The is a two program set which provides a means of balancing a closed traverse by either compass or transit rule. They are designed for use either with Closure (SY-06) or alone.

Given the sums of the latitudes and departures, regardless of sign, the end coordinates of the closure, and the end coordinates of the traverse leg, this program calculates the adjusted (balanced) coordinates. The transit rule, assumes that angular precision is generally higher than distancing precision.

  • If used standalone:
    • Initialize, press 2nd E'
    • Enter starting North, press 2nd A'
    • Enter starting East, press 2nd A'
    • Enter next North, press 2nd B'
    • Enter next East, press 2nd B'
    • Compute ∑|Lt| and ∑|Dp|, press 2nd C'
    • For next leg, re-start from step enter next North above.
    • Compute closure latitude and departure, press 2nd D'
    • Continue below...
  • If used immediatly after the Closure (SY-06), start here:
    • Enter starting North, press A
    • Enter starting East, press A
    • Enter unadjusted North, press B
    • Enter unadjusted East, press B
    • Compute adjusted North, press C
    • Compute adjusted East, press D
    • If more points, press E and re-start from step entering unadjusted North above.

Registers used: 01-07, 09, 11, 12, 14, 15, 18, 19
