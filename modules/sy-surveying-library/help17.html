This program calculates the coordinates of the point of intersection of two lines given the length of each line segment from a known point on that line to the point of intersection. Two solutions are possible. One is found by designating one line and its corresponding point and distance as line1, point1, and distance1, and the other as line2, etc... The other solution is found by reversing the designations and resolving the problem.

  • Select the degree mode, press 2nd Deg
  • Initialize, press 2nd E'
  • Enter N1 (point 1), press A
  • Enter E1 (point 1), press A
  • Enter N2 (point 2), press B
  • Enter E2 (point 2), press B
  • Enter Dist1, press C
  • Enter Dist2, press D
  • Compute angle between line1 and the line including point1 and point2, press E
  • Compute azimuth from point 1 to point 2, press 2nd A'
  • Compute, NI, press 2nd B'
  • Compute, EI, press 2nd C'

Registers used: 01-13, 20
