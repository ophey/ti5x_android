AV-18 — Rate of Climb; Turn Performance

As suggested by the title, this program contains two routines which operate independently.

Rate of Climb

Given the true airspeed and altitude change (+ or -), this routine can calculate the rate of climb (+ or -) if the ground distance over which the altitude change occurs is specified. Conversely, the distance can be calculated if the rate of climb is specified.

Turn Performance

Given the true airspeed, bank angle, and normal stall speed, this routine computes the G-force, turn diameter, time to complete a 360° turn, and the stall speed in the turn.

Bank= bank angle in degrees
Diam= turn diameter in n. miles
TAS= true airspeed in knots
t= time to complete a 360° turn (minutes))
Nstall= normal stall speed (knots)
Stall= stall speed in turn (knots)
G-force= force in direction of the turn radius expressed in parts of a G
