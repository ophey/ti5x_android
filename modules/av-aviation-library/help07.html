This program consists of two routines. Given a wind velocity and direction, an aircraft magnetic heading, and allowing for magnetic variation, the first routine calculates the tailwind and crosswind component vectors. And given several wind vectors along a flight path, the second routine calculates the approximate average wind vector for the entire flight path.
