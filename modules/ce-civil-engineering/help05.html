STR-05 - Hinged Portal

The program calculates the support reactions, the moments at the nodes and the moments in the position of the load in a symmetrical hinged portal. Three load conditions are foreseen: uniform load Q on the beam, force concentrated PV in any position A2 on the beam, force concentrated in any position A1 on the pillar. The section J of pillars and beam must be constant.

K = J2 ⁄ J1 . H ⁄ L
D = 2K + 3
B1 = H - A1
B2 = L - A2
α = A1 ⁄ H
β = 1 - α

Example

  1. Print option: 2nd St flg 1
  2. Set geometric data:
    • height, enter 300 cm, press A, display 300.
    • length, enter 500 cm, press B, display 500.
    • mom. inertia J1:, enter 160000 cm4, press C, display 160000.
    • mom. inertia J2:, enter 312500 cm4, press D, display 312500.
