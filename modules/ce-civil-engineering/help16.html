STR-16 - Rectangular Section in Reinforced Concrete - Press Bending Check

The program checks a rectangular section in reinforced concrete with symmetrical reinforcement, subject to pressure bending.

The conditions imposed by the operator are the stress in the tensioned irons and the ratio P between area compressed irons AFC and area taut iron AFT, according to the method of admissible stresses.

The units of measurement must be consistents and those of the International System: lengths in meters, forces in Newton and related multiples or submultiples.

The sign of the bending moment M and normal strength P is positive if it compresses the section (convention Science of the Constructions).

Instructions

  1. Provide the data

    Each input procedure is independent.

    Enter data and press the definable key. The program stores the data and displays previously stored data.

    • enter M (bending moment), press A.
    • enter N (normal strength in C point), press B.
    • enter B (section width), press C.
    • enter A (section height), press D.
    • enter h' (iron cover thickness), press E.
    • enter AF (symmetrical irons area), press 2ndA'.
    • enter G (homogenization coefficient), press 2ndB'.
    At the end, each procedure displays the replaced data.

    To view and correct a data, press its definable key twice.

    A first time to view it and correct it if necessary.

    A second time, to store it again in the same register.

  2. Results:

    Pressing 2nd E', the program display the neutral axis X calculated by solving the cubic equation with Newton's iterative method.

    In case of small eccentricity (M/N) < (A/6), the program stops flashing "-1".

    Pressing in sequence R/S will display the others results:

    ΣC (stress in the concrete),

    ΣFT (stress in tensioned irons),

    ΣFC (stress in compressed irons).

    The program will also print a report with data and results.

    For a new calculation, reset only the data that changes.

Example 1

Calculate a rectanguar section in reinforced concrete with the following data:

  • M=500000 dNcm
  • N=15000 dN
  • B=30 cm
  • A=40 cm
  • h’=3 cm
  • AF=6.03 cm²
  • G=10
  1. Set data:

    After entering the new data, the procedure displays the data previously stored.

    • M: enter 500000 dNcm, press A.
    • N: enter 15000 dN, press B.
    • B: enter 30 cm, press C.
    • A: enter 70 cm, press D.(*) data incorrect.
    • h': enter 3 cm, press E.
    • AF: enter 6.03 cm², press 2ndA'.
    • G: enter 10, press 2ndB'.
    • To display the section height "A": press D. Display = 70 (data incorrect),

      enter 40 cm, press D again to replace the previous incorrect data.

    • To display M: press A. Display = 500000 (data correct),

      press A to store it again.

  2. Results: press 2ndE'.Display = 14.47456442 cm (neutral axis X)

    Pressing in succession R/S will display the stresses ΣC, ΣFT and ΣFC (dN/cm²).

    The program will also print the following report

About The Program

Number of steps: from 000 to 426.

Registers used: 00 to 23

Flags used: 1

Labels: Eng, LNx, Adv, Prt, ẍ, π, -, +, and definable keys.
