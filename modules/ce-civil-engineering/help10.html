STR-10 — CIRCULAR PLATE

The program calculates the tangential and radial moment at the center (M,C), the tangential moment (MT,B) and radial moment (MR,B) at the edge, and the sag (F,C) in the center of a circular plate subjected to a uniform load. There are two types of constraint on the edge: support or perfect joint. The units of measurement of the forces and moments indicated in the figure must be those of the International System, forces in N (newton) or dN (decanewton=10N=1 kg); moments in kNm or kNcm (1 kNcm = 1 kgm).

Example

  1. Reset T−register: press CLR x⇌t
  2. Print option: press 2nd St flg 1
  3. Set data:
    • radius R: enter 200 cm, press A
    • thikness S: enter 25 cm, press B
    • distributed load Q: enter 0.1 dN/cm2, press C
    • elasticity modulus E: enter 200000 dN/cm2, press D
    • Poisson's ratio V: enter 0.1, press E
  4. Results:
    • support (APP): press 2nd A' 
            APP
            450.   MT,B
              0.   MR,B
            775.    M,C
        0.044064    F,C

    • joint (INC): press 2nd B' 
            INC
            -50.   MT,B
           -500.   MR,B
            275.    M,C
        0.009504    F,C

    Without print option each result will be displayed by pressing R/S in succession.

  5. For a new calculation, go back to step 4 if only the constraint or load data changes, else go back to step 3.

Registers used: 00 to 08

Flags used: 1

Labels: Prt, Op, Wrt, Lst, R/S, x, =, and definable keys.
