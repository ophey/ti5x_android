STR-09 — WEDGED RECTANGULAR PLATE

The program calculates the bending moment MX,B , MY,A along the edges A, B and MX,C, MY,C and the sag F,C in the center of a rectangular plate wedged on the edges and uniformly loaded on its surface. The units of measurement of the forces and moments indicated in the figure must be those of the International System, forces in N (newton) or dN (decanewton=10N=1 kg); moments in kNm or kNcm (1 kNcm = 1 kgm).

Example

  1. Reset T−register: press CLR x⇌t
  2. Print option: press 2nd St flg 1
  3. Set data:
    • side A: enter 300 cm, press A
    • side B: enter 500 cm, press B
    • thikness S: enter 15 cm, press C
    • elasticity modulus E: enter 200000 kg/cm2, press D
    • Poisson's ratio V: enter 0.1, press R/S
  4. Results:
    • set Q load: enter 0.04 dN/cm2, press E 
                  150.514818   MX,C
           40.23661655   MY,C
          -300.9152321   MX,B
          -164.1243553   MY,A
           0.014899079    F,C

    Without print option each result will be displayed by pressing R/S in succession.

  5. For a new calculation, go back to step 4 if only the load data changes, else go back to step 3.

Registers used: 00 to 12

Flags used: 1

Labels: Prt, Op, R/S, STO, and definable keys.
