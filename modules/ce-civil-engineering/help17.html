STR-17 - Rectangular Section in Reinforced Concrete - Press Bending Project

The program calculates the symmetrical reinforcement of a rectangular section in reinforced concrete subject to pressure bending.

The condition imposed by the operator is the maximum stress in the concrete.

The units of measurement must be consistents and those of the International System: lengths in meters, forces in Newton and related multiples or submultiples.

The sign of the bending moment M and normal strength N is positive if it compresses the section (convention Science of the Constructions).

Instructions

The instructions for using the program are summarized in the "card emulation”, similar to the magnetic card of the TI-59. It summarizes the instructions assigned to each definable key, transcribed in the box referring to the key.

  1. Provide the data:

    Each input procedure is independent.

    Enter data and press the definable key. The program stores the data and displays previously stored data.

    • enter M (bending moment), press A.
    • enter N (normal strength in C point), press B.
    • enter B (section width), press C.
    • enter A (section height), press D.
    • enter H'(iron cover thickness), press E.
    • enter ΣC (stress in tensioned irons), press 2nd A'.
    • enter G (homogenization coefficient), press 2nd B'.

    To view and correct a data, press its definable key twice. A first time to view it and correct it if necessary. A second time, to store it again in the same register.

  2. Results:

    Pressing 2nd E', the program display the neutral axis X calculated by solving the cubic equation with Newton-Raphson's iterative method.

    In case of small eccentricity (M/N)<(A/6), the program stops flashing "-1".

    Pressing in sequence R/S will display the others results:

    • AF (symmetrical irons area),
    • ΣFT (stress in tensioned irons),
    • ΣFC (stress in compressed irons).

    The program will also print a report with data and results.

    For a new calculation, reset only the data that changes.

Example 1

Calculate a rectanguar section in reinforced concrete with the following data:

  • M=600000 dNcm
  • N=12000 dN
  • B=30 cm
  • A=40 cm
  • H’=3 cm
  • ΣC=85 dN/cm²
  • G=10
  1. Set data:

    After entering the new data, the procedure displays the data previously stored.

    • M: enter 600000 dNcm, press A.
    • N: enter 12000 dN, press B.
    • B: enter 30 cm, press C.
    • A: enter 70 cm, press D.(*) data incorrect.
    • H': enter 3 cm, press E.
    • ΣC: enter 85 dN/cm², press 2ndA'.
    • G: enter 10, press 2ndB'.
    • To display the section height A: press D. Display = 70 (data incorrect),
      enter 40 cm, press D again to replace the previous incorrect data.
    • To display the bending moment "M": press A. Display = 600000 (data correct),
      press A to store it again.

  2. Results:

    Press 2ndE'. Display = 14.34657498 cm (neutral axis X)

    Pressing in succession R/S will display the stresses ΣFT, ΣFC (dN/cm²) and the symmetrical irons area AF (cm²).

    The program will also print the following report 

    --------------------

About the Program

Number of steps: from 000 to 479.

Registers used: 00 to 23

Flags used: 1

Labels: LNx, Deg, Adv, Prt, ẍ, π, -, +, and definable keys.
