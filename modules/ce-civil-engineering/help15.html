STR 15 — T-SECTION IN R.C.− BENDING PROJECT

The program calculates the area of the reinforcement in a reinforced concrete T section under bending stress. The conditions imposed by the operator are the stress in the taut iron and the ratio P between area compressed irons AFC and area taut iron AFT.

The units of measurement must be consistents and those of the International System: lengths in meters, forces in Newton and related multiples or submultiples. If the lengths are measured in cm and the forces in dN, the bending moment must be measured in (dNcm) and the stress in (dN/cm2).

The sign of the bending moment is positive (convention Science of the Constructions).

Instructions

The instructions for using the program are summarized in the card, similar to the magnetic card of the TI-59. It summarizes the instructions assigned to each definable key, transcribed in the box referring to the key.

  1. Provide the data:

    Each input procedure is independent.

    Enter data and press the definable key. The program stores the data and displays previously stored data.

    • enter M (bending moment), press A.
    • enter B (section width), press B.
    • enter B0 (central rib thickness), press C.
    • enter A (section height), press D.
    • enter A0 (upper wing thickness), press E.
    • enter H’(iron cover thickness), press 2ndA'.
    • enter G (homogenization coefficient), press 2ndB'.
    • enter ΣFT (stress in tensioned irons), press 2ndC'.
    • enter P = AFC/AFT, press 2ndD'.

    To view and correct a data, press its definable key twice.

    A first time to view it and correct it if necessary.

    A second time, to store it again in the same register.

  2. Results:

    Pressing 2nd E', the program, place B0=B and X=0, calculates a neutral axis X by successive approximations, considering a section rectangular. If X > A0 consider the T−section.

    Then display the neutral axis X.

    Pressing in sequence R/S will display the others results:

    ΣC (stress in the concrete),

    AFT (tensioned irons area),

    AFC (compressed irons area).

    The program will also print a report with data and results.

    For a new calculation, reset only the data that changes.

Example

Calculate a T section in reinforced concrete with the following data:

  • A = 60 cm
  • A0 =10 cm
  • B = 40 cm
  • B0 =20 cm
  • H’= 3 cm
  • G = 10
  • ΣAT = 1900 dN/cm²
  • P = AFC/AFT = 0.50
  • And subject to the bending moment M = 1200000 dNcm.

  1. Set data:
    • M: enter 1200000 dNcm, press A.
    • B: enter 80 cm, press B.
    • B0: enter 20 cm, press C.
    • A: enter 70 cm, press D.(*) data incorrect.
    • A0: enter 10 cm, press E.
    • H': enter 3 cm, press 2ndA'.
    • G: enter 10, press 2ndB'.
    • ∑FT: enter 1900 dN/cm², press 2ndC'.
    • "P": enter 0.5, press 2ndD'.

    • To display A: press D. Display = 70 (data incorrect),
      enter 60 cm, press D again to replace the previous incorrect data.
    • To display M: press A. Display = 1200000 (data correct),
      press A to store it again.
  2. Results: press 2ndE'. Display = 15.09369425 cm (neutral axis X)
    Pressing in succession R/S will display ΣC (dN/cm²), AFT and AFC (cm²).
    The program will also print the following report

About The Program

Number of steps: from 000 to 433.

Registers used: 00 to 19

Flags used: 1, 2

Labels: RCL, Prt, Op, LNx, Adv, ×, :, -, +, and definable keys.
