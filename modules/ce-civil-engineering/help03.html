STR-03 - Single Span Beams

The program calculates shear and bending moment in equidistant points whose number is set by the operator. There are four constraint conditions: left joint, 2 supports, left joint and right support, 2 joints. The loads, applicable separately or in combination, can be: moment at the extremity, force concentrated in any point, uniform load.

The four supoorted cases:

  • Left Joint:
  • Support - Support
  • Joint - Support
  • Joint - Joint
  1. Print option: 2nd St flg 1
  2. Provide data:
    • Beam length: enter L, press A
    • Concentrated load: enter P, press B
    • Load abscissa: enter E, press R/S
    • Uniform load: enter Q, press C
    • Moment to the left: enter Ms, press D
    • Moment to right: enter MD, press E
  3. Set the number of subdivisions of the beam, enter n, 2nd A'
  4. Select the constraint conditions and start calculation:
    • Left joint, 2nd B'
    • 2 supports, 2nd C'
    • Left joint and right support, 2nd D'
    • 2 joints, 2nd E'

    Results in "n+1" coordinates (from X0 to Xn = L), X0 is displayed.

    • X0 coordinated, M(X0) is displayed.
    • press R/S, T(X0) is displayed.
    • press R/S, X(X1) is displayed.
    • continues in the next coordinate X1 to Xn = L
  5. For a new calculation go back to step 2

Example

  1. Print option: 2nd St flg 1
  2. Provide data:
    • Beam length: enter 300, press A
    • Concentrated load: enter 500, press B
    • Load abscissa: enter 150, press R/S
    • Uniform load: enter 2, press C
  3. Set the number of subdivisions of the beam, enter 10, 2nd A'
  4. Select the constraint conditions and start calculation:
    • Left joint and right support, 2nd D'
