STR-08 — SUPPORTED RECTANGULAR PLATE

The program calculates the bending moments in both directions M(X), M(Y) and the sag F in the center of a rectangular plate resting on the edge and uniformly loaded on its surface. The units of measurement of the forces and moments indicated in the figure must be those of the International System, forces in N (newton) or dN (decanewton=10N=1 kg); moments in kNm or kNcm (1 kNcm = 1 kgm).

Example

  1. Reset T−register: press CLR x⇌t
  2. Print option: press 2nd St flg 1
  3. Set data:
    • side A:, enter 300 cm, press A
    • side B:, enter 500 cm, press B
    • thikness S:, enter 15 cm, press C
    • elasticity modulus E:, enter 200000 kg/cm2, press D
    • Poisson's ratio V:, enter 0.1, press R/S
  4. Results:
    • set Q load:, enter 0.1 dN/cm2, press E 
          760.0941417     M(X)
    292.0568407     M(Y)
    0.123557824        F

    • set Q load:, enter 0.12 dN/cm2, press E 
          912.1129791     M(X)
    350.4682184     M(Y)
    0.148269389        F

    Without print option each result will be displayed by pressing R/S in succession.

  5. For a new calculation, go back to step 4 if only the load data changes, else go back to step 3.

Registers used: 00 to 12

Flags used: 1

Labels: Prt, Op, R/S, = and definable keys A to E
