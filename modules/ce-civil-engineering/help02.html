STR-02 - Geometric Characteristics of Particular Section

The program sets a reference system and calculates the ordinate of the center of gravity YG and moment of inertia J/G with respect to a barycentric axis parallel to the x axis for:

Rectangular section:

T section:

Circular section:

  1. Print option:  2nd   St flg   1 
  2. Rectangular section:
    • Recall height A, press  A , if different enter A and press  R/S .
    • Recall width B, press  B , if different enter B and press  R/S .
    • Start calculation, press  2nd   A' , YG is displayed, press  R/S  J/G is displayed.
  3. T section:
    • Recall total height A, press  A , if different enter A and press  R/S .
    • Recall upper wing width B, press  B , if different enter B and press  R/S .
    • Recall upper wing thickness A0, press  C , if different enter A0 and press  R/S .
    • Recall central rib thickness B0, press  D , if different enter B0 and press  R/S .
    • Start calculation, press  2nd   B' , YG is displayed, press  R/S  J/G is displayed.
  4. Circular section:
    • Recall diameter D, press  E , if different enter D and press  R/S .
    • Start calculation, press  2nd   C' , YG is displayed, press  R/S  J/G is displayed.

Flags used:

  • 1 - enable print

Registers used: 0 to 8.
