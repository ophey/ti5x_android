STR 12 — Rectangular Section In R.C. − Bending Check

The program performs the bending check of a rectangular section in R.C. (reinforced concrete), according to the method of admissible stresses.

The units of measurement must be consistents and those of the International System: lengths in meters, forces in Newton and related multiples or submultiples. If the lengths are measured in cm and the forces in dN, the bending moment must be measured in (dNcm) and the stress in (dN/cm²).

The sign of the bending moment is positive (convention Science of the Constructions).

Instructions

1 - Provide the data: Each input procedure is independent.

  • enter M (bending moment), press A (display= previously stored data).
  • enter B (section width), press B (display= previously stored data).
  • enter A (section height), press C (display= previously stored data).
  • enter H’ (steel cover thickness), press C (display= previously stored data).
  • enter G (homogenization coefficient), press D (display= previously stored data).
  • enter AFT (tensioned irons area), press 2ndA' (display= previously stored data).
  • enter AFC (compressed irons area), press 2ndB' (display= previously stored data).

To view a data, press its definable key twice. A first time to view it and correct it if necessary. A second time, to store it again in the same register.

2 - Results:

  • Pressing 2ndE', the program will display the neutral axis X.
  • Pressing in succession R/S will display the others results ΣC (max. stress in the concrete) and ΣFT (max. stress in tensioned irons)
  • For a new calculation, reset only the data that changes.

Example 1

1 - Set data:

  • M: enter 500000 dNcm, press A (display= previously stored data).
  • B: enter 30 cm, press B (display= previously stored data).
  • A: enter 50 cm, press C (display= previously stored data).
  • H': enter 3 cm, press D (display= previousred data).
  • G: enter 10, press E (display= previously stored data).
  • AFT: enter 12.06 cm², press 2ndA' (display= previously stored data)
  • AFC: enter 6.03 cm², press 2ndB' (display= previously stored data)
2 - Results: press 2ndE'.
  • Display = 14.61705548 cm (neutral axis X)
  • Pressing in succession R/S will display ΣC (dN/cm²) and ΣFT (dN/cm²)
  • For a new calculation, reset only the data that changes.
  • The program will also print the following report
