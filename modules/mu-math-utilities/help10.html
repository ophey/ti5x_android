The program will compute the values of all the hyperbolic functions and the inverse hyperbolic functions.

The sorted numbers are stored sequentially in the data registers beginning with R01.

  • For sinh, press A
  • For sinh-1, press 2nd A'
  • For cosh, press B
  • For cosh-1, press 2nd B'
  • For tanh, press C
  • For tanh-1, press 2nd C'

Registers: none
