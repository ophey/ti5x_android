Given the open-loop poles and zeros of a linear feedback system, this program calculates the following root-locus parameters: asymptote intersection point, asymptote angles, departure angles from complex poles, and arrival angles at complex zeros.

  • Initialize program : SBR CLR
  • Enter number of poles, press n 2nd A'
  • Enter real part of 1st pole, press A
  • Enter imag. part of 1st pole, press R/S

    Repeat both steps above until n poles have been entered.

  • Enter number of zeros, press m 2nd B'
  • Enter real part of 1st zero, press A
  • Enter imag. part of 1st zero, press R/S

    Repeat both steps above until m zeros have been entered.

  • To Calculate asymptote intersection, press C, then press R/S to calculate the (n-m) asymptome angles.
  • To Calculate departure angles from poles, enter J (1 .. n) and press D
  • To Calculate arrival angles at zeros, enter K (1 .. m) and press E
