LE-04 FOOTBALL PREDICTOR

This program predicts the score of a football game based on four factors:

  1. Average of points scored by the home team's offense, HO
  2. Average of points scored against the home team's defense, HD
  3. Average of points scored by the opposing team's offense, OO
  4. Average of points scored by the opposing team's defense, OD

The predicted score is determined in the following manner:

Home Team's Score = (HO+OD) / 2

Opposing Team's Score = (HD+OO) / 2

Because the predictions are based on averages, the scores predicted tend to fall within a limited range and extreme scores never appear.

INSTRUCTIONS (Display Only)

  1. Press 2nd Pgm04 SBR CLR to initialize; 0. is displayed.

  2. For each game the home team has played:

    1. Enter points scored by the home team and press B.

    2. Enter points scored against the home team and press R/S.


  3. For each game the opposing team has played:

    1. Enter points scored by the opposing team and press 2nd D'.

    2. Enter points scored against the opposing team and press R/S.


  4. Make Prediction by pressing E. Predicted score is displayed in the following format:

    Home Team Score . Opposing Team Score
