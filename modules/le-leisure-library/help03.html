This program computes the correct d-stop when flash is used in the presence of strong ambient light, such as when flash is used outdoors to fill-in shadows. It may be used with automatic or nonautomatic flash units. For nonautomatic flash, the required working distance, given the desired f-stop, is also computed.

  • Initialize program : SBR CLR
  • Enter the number of f-stops, N1, subject is underexposed without fill in and press A. Lighting ratio, 2N1, is displayed. N1 must be greater than N2.
  • Enter desired lighting ration, 2N2, and press B. Desired lighting ration is displayed.
  • For automatic flash, enter the auto f-setting and press C.
  • For nonautomatic flash, enter the guide number and press C. Then
    • To solve the working distance, enter the desired f-number and press D.
    • To solve the f-number, enter the desired working distance and press D.
