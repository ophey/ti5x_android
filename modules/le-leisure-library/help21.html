Each person's "good" and "bad" days are said to be patterned in part by physical, emotional, and intellectual biorhythm cycles which start at birth and continue throughout his life. Using this program you can determine your position in the biorhythm cycles on any given day simply by entering your birthdate and the date in question.

The relative match of one person's "ups and downs" to another's is called biorhythm compatibility.

  • 50% is avarage
  • below 35% indicates potential for strong variances
  • above 65% indicates potential for blissful relations

Date are entered in the form MMDD.YYYY, where MM is the month number, DD is the day and YYYY is the year. For example, December 7, 1941 is entered as 1207.1941.

Biorhythm Chart

  • Press SBR CLR to initialize program.
  • Key in birth date (MMDD.YYYY), press A.
  • Key in test date, press B. The number in the display indicates the day of the week of the test date where 0=Saturday, 1=Sunday, 2=Monday..., 6=Friday.
  • Press press 2nd E' to chart your biorhythm cycles. The numbers down the center of the tape locate the critical points and correspond to the days of the week as described above. "P" is plotted for the physical cycle, "E" for emotional and "I" for intellectual. An "X" is printed where two cyles share the same point. Press R/S to stop the program.

Biorhythm Cycles

  • Press SBR CLR to initialize program.
  • Key in birth date (MMDD.YYYY), press A.
  • Key in test date, press B.
  • Display the following values:
    • Press C to determine amplitude of physical cycle.
    • Press D to determine amplitude of emotional cycle.
    • Press C to determine amplitude of intellectual cycle.

Biorhythm Compatibility

  • Press SBR CLR to initialize program.
  • Key in earlier birth date (MMDD.YYYY), press A.
  • Key in later birth date (MMDD.YYYY), press B. Day of the week of later birth date is displayed (see above).
  • Press 2nd C' to determine biorhythm compatibility.
