ML-20 — Days Of The Week, Days Between Dates

This program calculates the number of days between any two Gregorian calendar dates after 1582. It can also calculate the day of the week corresponding to a date.

To compute the number of days between two dates:

  • Enter the first date: mmdd.yyyy A
  • Enter the second date: mmdd.yyyy B
  • Display the number of days in-between: C

You can re-enter either of the first two values while keeping the existing value of the other one, then press C to compute the new number of days.

To compute the day of week corresponding to a date:

  • Enter the date: mmdd.yyyy then press D to display a number in the range [0 .. 6], with 0 representing Saturday, 1 sunday .. 6 representing Friday.

Formulas Used

Each date is converted to a “FACTOR” (integer number of days from an arbitrary reference point) using the following formulae, depending on the month:

For January and February:

FACTOR = 365 × YYYY + DD + 31 × (MM - 1) + Int[(YYYY - 1) ÷ 4] - Int(Int[(YYYY - 1) ÷ 100 + 1] × 3 ÷ 4) - 1

For March and later months:

FACTOR = 365 × YYYY + DD + 31 × (MM - 1) - Int(0.4 × MM + 2.3) + Int(YYYY ÷ 4) - Int([Int(YYYY ÷ 100) + 1] × 3 ÷ 4) - 1

The day of the week can be computed from the FACTOR as follows:

Day of Week = FACTOR + Int(- FACTOR ÷ 7) × 7

Registers used: 01-05.
