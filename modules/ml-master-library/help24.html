ML-24 — Unit Conversions (1)

This program does conversions between various linear units of measurement. Enter the value in the units to convert from, and press the appropriate conversion key to display the value in the units to convert to, as follows:

  • inches to centimetres: A
  • centimetres to inches: 2nd A´
  • feet to metres: B
  • metres to feet: 2nd B´
  • yards to metres: C
  • metres to yards: 2nd C´
  • miles to kilometres: D
  • kilometres to miles: 2nd D´
  • nautical miles to miles: E
  • miles to nautical miles: 2nd E´

Registers used: none.
